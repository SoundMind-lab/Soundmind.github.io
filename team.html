Meet Our Team

Dr. Shruti Rajendra Kshirsagar (Assistant Professor)

Dr. Shruti Rajendra Kshirsagar is an Assistant Professor at Wichita State University. She holds a PhD in Telecommunications with a specialization in Speech & Deep Learning from INRS, Montreal, Canada. Dr. Kshirsagar's research interests include Data Science, Deep Learning, Machine Learning, Speech Signal Processing, Affective Computing, and more. She has extensive experience in both academia and industry, having worked as an Audio R&D Scientist and a Machine Learning for Audio Analytics Intern.
